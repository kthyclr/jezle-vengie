Good to Know

FAQs

  • How do I get to the ceremony venue?

    The ceremony is at San Vicente Ferrer Quasi Parish, Pasian, Monkayo, Davao de Oro. From Davao City, take the highway towards Monkayo (approx. 2–3 hours). Look for Pasian barangay and the parish church is prominently visible from the main road. We recommend arriving at least 20–30 minutes early.

    View full venue details & directions →

  • Are plus ones allowed?

    Our celebration is intimate, so we kindly ask that only those formally invited attend. If your invitation includes a plus one, it will be indicated on your invite. Thank you for understanding.

  • What is the dress code?

    We're going for a warm Brown Palette — earthy tones, tans, caramels, and creams. Please avoid white and ivory out of respect for the bride. For full details and outfit inspiration, see our Dress Code guide →

  • What time does the reception end?

    The reception and program are expected to wrap up by 3:00 PM. We'll update this page if the schedule changes, so check back closer to the date!
